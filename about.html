About Logic Battle

A competitive community for Logic Pro beatmakers.

Mission

Our goal is to create a level playing field for producers. By providing a curated set of loops and samples for every battle, we ensure that the focus remains on creative arrangement, sound design, and engineering skills.

Fairness Model

Every competitor receives the exact same official loop pack. Outcomes reflect true ability rather than who has the most expensive sample libraries.

Logic Pro Compatibility

While we celebrate all DAWs, our submission pipeline is optimized for Logic Pro. We encourage users to share channel strips and project notes to help others learn.

Battle Flow

Discover an active battle, download the pack, produce your track, and upload your high-quality bounce. The community then votes to determine the winner.
