About Logic Battle

Beat-battle style community built for Logic Pro producers.

Fairness Model

Every competitor receives the same official loop pack so outcomes reflect arranging, sound design, mixing, and creativity.

Logic Pro Compatibility

Submission pipeline is designed for Logic Pro projects with required WAV bounces and optional stem prints for portability.

Battle Flow

Discover battle → download pack → produce in Logic → upload files → community voting → leaderboard points.

Logic Battle prototype modeled after beat-battle flow, adapted for Logic Pro-compatible submissions.
